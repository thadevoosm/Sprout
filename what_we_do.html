Supply & Distribution

At Sprout, we're committed to providing our clients with high-quality raw materials for biodegradable paper cup production. Our supply and distribution services include:

Raw Material Supply

We provide a wide range of raw materials for biodegradable paper cup production, including paperboard, aquatic coated paper cups fans, and plant-based biodegradable materials. Our raw materials are sourced from reputable suppliers to ensure the highest quality and sustainability standards.

Water-Based Coated Paper Distribution

We distribute water-based coated, non-plastic coated paper for use in paper cup production. Our paper is made from renewable materials and is designed to provide a high-quality, eco-friendly alternative to traditional plastic-coated paper cups.

Biodegradable Paper Cup Distribution

We also distribute biodegradable paper cups for the local and international markets. Our paper cups are made from plant-based materials and are 100% biodegradable, making them a sustainable and eco-friendly choice for food service providers.

Customized Solutions

We work closely with our clients to provide customized solutions that meet their specific needs. Whether you need raw materials, water-based coated paper, biodegradable paper cups, or a combination of these products, we can help you find the right solution for your business.
