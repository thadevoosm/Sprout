Sprout Group Wins CII National Awards for Environmental Best Practices Trophy and Certificate 2021

New Delhi, India - The Confederation of Indian Industries (CII) organized the CII Environmental Best Practices Award on 28, 29 & 30 July 2021 to recognize, encourage, and support the efforts of Indian industries in the conservation of natural resources and pollution prevention. Awards were granted to units that made a significant and measurable contribution towards the development or use of cleaner technologies, processes, or practices for environmental protection.

Kerala Vyapari Vyavasayi Ekopana Samithi Urges Retailers to Use Biodegradable Paper Cups in Wake of Plastic Ban

The Kerala Vyapari Vyavasayi Ekopana Samithi has released a press notice urging all retailers and distributors in Kerala to use Sprout Greenpak paper cups, which are biodegradable, home compostable, and recyclable. The notice comes in the wake of a ban on single-use plastic products in Kerala since January 2020. The association strictly prohibits the sale of any banned single-use paper products and will not offer any assistance to those caught selling them. The association is promoting the use of only eco-friendly products and is asking all sellers in Kerala to sell only biodegradable paper cups

Maldives Takes a Bold Step to Ban Single-Use Plastics from 2023 to Save its Fragile Ecosystem

The Maldives government has taken a bold step in the fight against plastic pollution by announcing a nationwide ban on single-use plastics from 2023. The ban is part of the country's efforts to protect its fragile ecosystem and promote sustainable tourism. The Maldives, a nation of 1,200 islands, is known for its pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant marine life. However, the country's tourism industry, which is the main source of income, has also contributed to the growing problem of plastic pollution. According to a study conducted by the World Bank, the Maldives generates around 330 tons of plastic waste per day, with 90% of it ending up in the ocean.
