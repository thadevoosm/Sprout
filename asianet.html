Managing Director of Sprout Group Interviewed by Asianet News Network at Global Expo on Waste Management Technologies.

Sprout Group's managing director, Sandeep Philip, was recently interviewed by Asianet News Network Pvt. Ltd. at the Global Expo on Waste Management Technologies held on 4th, 5th, and 6th February 2023 at Marine Drive, Kochi. The event was organized by Suchitwa Mission for the Local Self Government Department, Government of Kerala, and provided an incredible opportunity for Sprout Group to showcase their alternative products for single-use plastics. In the interview, Sandeep Philip highlighted the commitment of Sprout Group towards finding sustainable solutions for environmental challenges. He stated that the company has been working towards creating innovative products that have the potential to revolutionize the way we consume and dispose of single-use plastics. During the event, the Sprout Group team was able to connect with experts, industry leaders, and policymakers, and they had the opportunity to showcase their products, which are biodegradable, home compostable, and recyclable. The products include paper cups, plates, straws, and other eco-friendly alternatives to single-use plastics. The Global Expo on Waste Management Technologies was a huge success, and it provided an ideal platform for Sprout Group to share their ideas and solutions. The event was attended by various stakeholders, including government officials, industry experts, and the general public. The organizers of the event were praised for their tireless efforts in bringing together a diverse group of people and organizations to work towards a cleaner and greener future. Sandeep Philip expressed his gratitude to Suchitwa Mission for giving Sprout Group the opportunity to present their ideas and solutions. He stated that the event was a great success and that it would not have been possible without the tireless efforts of the organizers and volunteers who made it happen. Sprout Group's commitment towards finding sustainable solutions for environmental challenges is commendable, and their participation in events like the Global Expo on Waste Management Technologies is a testament to their dedication. They are setting an example for other companies to follow, and it is hoped that more companies will follow suit and work towards creating a better, cleaner, and more sustainable world.
