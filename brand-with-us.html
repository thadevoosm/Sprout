Sustainable Paper Products and Packaging for Your Eco-Friendly Business Needs

At Sprout, we're committed to the environment, which is why we offer a range of sustainably sourced paper products and packaging. Our environmentally friendly products include not just paper cups, but also paper straws, bags, and more, all made from sustainably managed forestry and certified by PEFC. Our cups are coated with a water-based emulsion as per government regulations, making them 100% biodegradable, recyclable, and compostable. To further reduce our environmental impact, we use only water-based inks for printing our custom-branded paper cups and stock designs. If you're looking for eco-friendly alternatives to traditional paper products and packaging that perfectly represent your business, get in touch with our team today.
