Why Us?

Plastic Free

Our CIPET Plastic Free certification ensures seamless compliance with Europe's single-use plastic directives, showcasing our commitment to sustainability and environmental stewardship.

Sustainable

Our sustainable products are crafted from responsibly sourced paper, preserving forests and promoting an environmentally conscious production cycle.

Recyclable

Certified by India's CPPRI, our products are fully recyclable and repulpable, supporting our commitment to sustainability and contributing to a circular economy.

Compostable

Our products are home compostable, enhancing convenience and promoting an eco-friendly waste management system for sustainable living.

No Harmful Chemicals

Our products prioritize safety and the environment by being free from harmful chemicals like PFAS and OBA, ensuring non-toxic options for our customers.

Food Safe

Our products meet strict international food safety standards, guaranteeing compliance and customer confidence in their safety for consumption.

Microwavable

Our products excel in heat resistance, withstanding temperatures of up to 230 degrees Celsius for one hour, fully compliant with EU regulation 1935/2004.

Water-Based

By opting for aqueous coating instead of plastic coating, we provide an eco-friendly alternative that preserves product quality and minimizes our environmental footprint.

Our Biodegradable Products

Our plastic free disposable products are biodegradable. They leave no trace on our environment and they are home compostable too. You don't need any industrial facility to compost our products. Moreover, You don't have to worry about collecting them back from the beaches, valleys, or streets because they are going to decompose or degrade naturally. You know why? Simply because our products do not have a plastic coating.

Make our mother nature Happy. Our products do not harm your health or pollute our wonderful Earth!

Did you Know?

Plastic coated paper cups are made of polyethylene, a petroleum by-product & the same stuff that fuels our vehicles.

All plastics including bio-plastics, plant-plastics, and PLA plastics are harmful to the marine environment.

Each day we use 500,000,000 plastic coated paper cups;enough to fill,46,400 large school busses per year.

Plastic coated paper cups are among the top 10 debris items.44% of all seabirds & mammals have ingested plastic.
