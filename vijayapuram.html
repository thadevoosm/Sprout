Vijayapuram Social Service Society collaborates with Startups to promote Biodegradable Products

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Vijayapuram in Kerala has been actively working towards socio-economic and educational development interventions for the past 61 years through its social apostolate - Vijayapuram Social Service Society (VSSS). The organization has been striving for the integral development of the poor and marginalized people in the operational area and has collaborated with various agencies to fight for ecological balance and restoration. As part of their commitment towards ecological issues, VSSS has collaborated with two new startups - SPROUT GROUP and THOOSAN to replace plastic with biodegradable material products like carry bags, straws, plates, etc. The collaboration aims to promote sustainable development and ecological balance in the society. To promote awareness among the younger generation, VSSS organized a competition among Balaveedi Students on the need for replacing plastic with degradable materials. The event was held on August 13, 2022, at the VSSS auditorium, Mount Carmel church campus, and the winners were awarded cash prizes of Rs.3,000, Rs.2,000, and Rs.1,000 for the first, second, and third prize, respectively. SPROUT GROUP and THOOSAN are new startups supported by international and national agencies to bring a cutlery revolution in the society for an eco-friendly atmosphere. They aim to provide a replacement for the current disposable plastic utensils on the market, which end up in landfills every year after being used once. To promote their mission, VSSS in collaboration with SPROUT GROUP and THOOSAN organized a product launch event named KSHITHI 2022. The event provided a platform to showcase the alternative products for single-use plastics, and the startups were able to connect with experts, industry leaders, and policymakers. The collaboration between VSSS, SPROUT GROUP, and THOOSAN is a significant step towards promoting sustainable development and ecological balance. The initiative will go a long way in creating a better, cleaner, and more sustainable world for future generations.
