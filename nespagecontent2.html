Sprout Group's Biodegradable Solution Accepted for The SUP Challenge - Goa

Sprout Group's innovative solution to the problem of single-use plastic waste has been selected for The SUP Challenge - Goa. The challenge, organized by The Incubation Network and funded by the German Federal Ministry for Economic Cooperation and Development (BMZ) and ECCA Family Foundation, aims to accelerate startups in South and Southeast Asia to pilot their solutions with the F&B industry to reduce single-use plastic waste
