Sprout Group Wins CII National Awards for Environmental Best Practices Trophy and Certificate 2021

New Delhi, India - The Confederation of Indian Industries (CII) organized the CII Environmental Best Practices Award on 28, 29 & 30 July 2021 to recognize, encourage, and support the efforts of Indian industries in the conservation of natural resources and pollution prevention. Awards were granted to units that made a significant and measurable contribution towards the development or use of cleaner technologies, processes, or practices for environmental protection. Sprout Group, a leading paper products manufacturer, has been awarded two prestigious CII National Awards for Environmental Best Practices Trophy and Certificate 2021 for the Most Innovative Environmental Project and the Most Useful Environmental Project. The awards were given in recognition of Sprout Group's pioneering sustainability initiatives in the Biodegradable Paper Cup and Containers project (manufacturing and distribution). The award was received by Mr. Sandeep Philip, Managing Director, on behalf of Sprout Group, at the 8th Greenco Summit organized virtually by the Confederation of Indian Industry (CII). Sprout Group was shortlisted for the national level competition among 36 companies selected from two hundred Indian industries to showcase the best shortlisted projects from areas such as Resource & Material Conservation, Waste Management & Recycling, Adoption of the latest Pollution Control Measures, Climate Change & Mitigation efforts, Greening the Supply Chain, Product Responsibility Management, Life Cycle Assessment, and Biodiversity Management. The project presented by Sprout Group before the esteemed panel of jury included a solution for the single-use plastic-coated paper cups and food containers. The panel was pleased and appreciated the project of biodegradable paper cups and containers. Mr. L S Ganapati, Chairman, CII National Award for Environmental Best Practices 2021, commented on the project as a unique one and one of the best alternatives for single use plastic pollution. Sprout Group has always been committed to adopting the best and environment friendly practices that ensure minimal impact on the community. This prestigious award bears testimony to our efforts towards environmental innovation for a greener tomorrow. Sprout Group is the only company in Kerala that has government approval to manufacture biodegradable paper cups at present. The CII Environmental Best Practices Award is a significant recognition for Sprout Group's contribution to the environment, and the company will continue to strive towards creating innovative and sustainable solutions to protect the planet.
