SPROUT GROUP LAUNCHES INNOVATIVE ECO-FRIENDLY PACKAGING SOLUTIONS: SPROUT GREENPAK

Kochi, India - Sprout Group, a leading manufacturer of paper products, has launched its innovative eco-friendly packaging solutions under the brand name Sprout Greenpak. The official launching ceremony of Sprout Greenpak was held at Crowne Plaza in Kochi on March 21, 2023.

The event was graced by Shri. Hibi Eden MP, Loksabha, Kochi, and Adv. M. Anil Kumar, Mayor, Kochi, as chief guests. The ceremony was attended by several prominent personalities from the business community and government officials. Sprout Greenpak is a range of eco-friendly packaging solutions that includes paper cups, food containers, and other packaging materials made from biodegradable and compostable materials. The products are designed to reduce the use of plastic and promote sustainable packaging practices.

Speaking at the event, Mr. Sandeep Philip, Group Head-Managing and Operations, Sprout Group, said, "We are delighted to launch Sprout Greenpak, our latest range of eco-friendly packaging solutions. We have always been committed to adopting the best and environmentally friendly practices that ensure minimal impact on the community. With Sprout Greenpak, we aim to offer innovative and sustainable packaging solutions that not only meet the needs of our customers but also protect the planet."

Speaking on the occasion, Shri. Hibi Eden MP, Loksabha, Kochi, said, "I am delighted to see young entrepreneurs taking up the cause of sustainability and contributing to the larger goal of environmental conservation. I urge all the stakeholders in the food and beverage industry to adopt such eco-friendly packaging solutions and contribute towards a greener future."

Adv.M.Anil Kumar, Mayor, Kochi, added, "I congratulate the team behind Sprout Greenpak for their innovative and sustainable business model. Kochi has always been at the forefront of promoting eco-friendly initiatives, and I am happy to see such ventures being launched in our city. I am sure that Sprout Greenpak will inspire many other businesses to adopt sustainable practices."

The launching ceremony of Sprout Greenpak was a resounding success, and it marked a significant milestone in the journey of the Sprout Group towards a sustainable future. The event showcased the growing interest and awareness among businesses towards sustainable practices and the need to reduce their impact on the environment
